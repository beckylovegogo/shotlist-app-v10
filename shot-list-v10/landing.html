The Problem

Most creators
shoot blind

No shot plan. No structure. You arrive on location and realize you have no idea what to film — then spend hours in editing trying to fix a broken story.

01
No shot plan
Most creators skip planning entirely, relying on instinct — and miss crucial shots they only realize they needed in the edit.
02
Camera language is hard
Close-up, medium shot, pan, push-in — knowing when and how to use each technique takes years of experience to internalize.
03
Planning takes too long
Writing a proper shot list manually is time-consuming. So most people skip it and pay the price in the edit room.
The Solution

Your AI director
on standby

Pocket Director generates a complete, professional shot list in seconds. Then guides you through each shot on set with an AR overlay — so you never miss a frame.

🤖
AI Shot List Generation
Powered by Gemini AI. Enter your topic and video format — get 6 to 15 professional shots with shot size, camera movement, and visual description.
🎬
Plan A and Plan B
Every generation produces two different shot list approaches. Pick the one that fits your vision — or switch between them on set.
📱
AR Filming Mode
Once you confirm your shot list, the app opens your camera with each shot details overlaid on screen. Record, advance, repeat — no missed shots.
🌐
Bilingual — No Install Required
Works in English and Traditional Chinese. Built as a PWA — open it in any mobile browser, no app store needed.
